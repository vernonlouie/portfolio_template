About Me

I'm a former engineer and stay-at-home-dad who's now channeling into web development. I had taken courses/short classes in computer science in college and during my work as an engineer and found I liked the logic, structure and game of trying to make the briefest, but still understandable code. So I ran through the HTML, CSS, Javascript and jQuery modules on Codecademy and later enrolled at LearningFuze Accelerated Web Development School and developed more skills (see below). I can now point at things on a laptop or phone and say that I made that happen!

Though it still needs some work, I'm most proud of the MacDIYver app, because it was the app that combined back end with front end and we made it work as a team. Users input what tools they have and the app shows which art/home projects they can make with those tools. Users can sign in, submit projects, rate projects, comment, buy their project to be featured, and even search the neighborhood for tools. People like the MacDIYver idea and have said that they would definitely use this app. I'm ready to rock!
