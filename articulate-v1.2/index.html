About Me

I transitioned from aerospace engineer to stay-at-home-dad and now to web development. I grew up in San Francisco and moved to the LA/OC metropolitan area in 2003. I had taken courses/short classes in computer science in college and during my work as an engineer and found I liked the logic, structure and game of trying to make the briefest, but still understandable code.

As I reenter the workforce, I thought I would give web development my best shot as people have said I definitely have the mind for it. So I ran through the HTML, CSS, Javascript and jQuery modules on Codecademy and later enrolled at LearningFuze Accelerated Web Development School and developed more skills (see below). I can now point at things on a laptop or phone and say that I made that happen! Mind-boggling! I'm ready to rock!!
