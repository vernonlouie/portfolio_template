About Me

I am transitioning from aerospace engineer to stay-at-home-dad to web developer. I moved from San Francisco to the LA/OC metropolitan area not knowing if I would return to the San Francisco Bay Area. I had taken courses/short classes in computer science in college and during my work as an engineer and found I liked the logic, structure and game of trying to make the briefest, but still understandable code. As I reenter the workforce, I thought I would give web development my best shot as people have said I definitely have the mind for it. So I ran through the HTML, CSS, Javascript and jQuery modules on Codecademy and found a passion reinvigorated. I enrolled at LearningFuze Accelerated Web Development School, developed more skills (see below) and am ready to rock!!

Looking at opportunities in Orange County and San Gabriel Valley (LA county).
